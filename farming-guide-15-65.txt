===================================================
  OSRS LEAGUES - FARMING GUIDE: LEVEL 15 → 65
===================================================

SETUP
-----
- 1 Tree patch, 1 Fruit tree patch
- 8x XP multiplier (league bonus)
- 5x faster growth (league bonus)

XP NEEDED
---------
Level 15 = 2,411 XP
Level 65 = 449,428 XP
Total to gain: 447,017 XP

===================================================
  TREE XP REFERENCE (with 8x bonus)
===================================================

NORMAL TREES (grow times are 5x faster)
  Oak     (lvl 15) | 3,850.4 XP  | 32 min grow
  Willow  (lvl 30) | 11,852 XP   | 48 min grow
  Maple   (lvl 45) | 27,587.2 XP | 64 min grow
  Yew     (lvl 60) | 57,207.2 XP | 80 min grow

FRUIT TREES (all 192 min / 3h 12m grow, 5x faster)
  Apple      (lvl 27) | 10,180 XP
  Banana     (lvl 33) | 14,732 XP    <-- SKIPPED*
  Orange     (lvl 39) | 20,693.6 XP  <-- SKIPPED*
  Curry      (lvl 42) | 24,295.2 XP
  Pineapple  (lvl 51) | 38,333.6 XP
  Papaya     (lvl 57) | 51,043.2 XP

*Banana and Orange are skipped entirely. By the time the fruit
 patch is free for a second run, you're already level 44 and
 curry (lvl 42) gives more XP than either.

===================================================
  SEEDS NEEDED (TOTAL)
===================================================

  With supercompost and no farmer payment, each tree has roughly
  a 10% chance of dying per run. Insurance assumes: if one dies,
  the replacement will survive (no double-death budgeting).

  Normal Trees:
    Oak saplings    x3  (+1)  -- 27% chance at least one of 3 dies
    Willow saplings x4  (+1)  -- 34% chance at least one of 4 dies
    Maple saplings  x6  (+2)  -- 47% chance at least one of 6 dies; nearly a coin flip, definitely bring one
    Yew saplings    x2  (+1)  -- 19% chance; low odds but yew takes 80 min so losing one hurts

  Fruit Trees:
    Apple sapling     x1  (+1)  -- 10% chance; low but a failed run costs 3h 12m
    Curry sapling     x1  (+1)  -- 10% chance; same logic
    Pineapple sapling x1  (+1)  -- 10% chance; same logic
    Papaya sapling    x1  (+1)  -- 10% chance; this is your final critical run, always bring a spare
