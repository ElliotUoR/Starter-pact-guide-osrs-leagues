Hunter rumours are like slayer tasks assigned to you by guild hunters in the Burrow (https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/The_Burrow)
To begin first talk to Guild Scribe Verity (https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Guild_Scribe_Verity) in the Burrow (https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/The_Burrow) under the Hunter Guild (https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Hunter_Guild)

Unlike slayer tasks where you are given a fixed number of kills, hunter rumours can finish at any point, with a maximum limit until completing the rumour.
You can effectively 'block' tasks from being given; for example if you talk to the novice hunter and he gives you a pyre fox task, you can talk to another hunter 
e.g an adept hunter; for another task and it cannot be a pyre fox task because the novice hunter is 'holding' that task.

This can be setup in leagues by only having varlamore unlock to always give red chinchompa tasks. See the following setups for different difficulties below.

Novice:
need level 46 hunter for first rumours, spam novice rumours until lvl 57

Adept:
at 57 use ornus until pyre fox task
now use cervus for always red chins

Expert:
at 72 use aco until sunlight antelope
then use teco for red chins
at 75 sunlight moths can be assigned
use cervus to block sunlight moths
then continue with red chins


ALTERNATE at 72
block tecu salamander with gilman (novice)
block sunlight antelope with teco
result: you can switch between red chins and moonlight moths (can block red chins with an adept for full butterfly)
.



Masters offer 2 more loot rolls but will take time to setup (and you cannot fully do red chins)

Block moonlight antelope with gilman (novice)
